sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: It ran faster with a sorted list but not as fast as the alleged merge sort.

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: It ran faster than the other two. It also seemed to scale better with larger lists.

sort3 uses: Selection sort

How do you know?: It ran in Theta(n^2). It took just as long with the random text as with the sorted text.
